What is Tic-Tac-Toe?

Tic-Tac-Toe (also known as Noughts and Crosses or X's and O's) is a paper-and-pencil game for two players, X and O, who take turns marking the spaces in a 3Ã—3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game. (Wikipedia)

The Tic-Tac-Toe game is as follows:

  1. A two player game

  2. Player "X" and "O" will take turns to play the game (click on the buttons).

  3. The game ends when either one player wins, or all buttons are clicked without a winner.
